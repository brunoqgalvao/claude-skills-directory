Overview

Streamline your customer support workflow with intelligent ticket classification and routing.

Features

  • Auto Classification: Categorize tickets by issue type, urgency, and sentiment
  • Smart Routing: Route to the right team based on content analysis
  • Priority Detection: Identify urgent issues automatically
  • Reason Logging: Document routing decisions for audit trails

Integrations

  • Zendesk
  • Freshdesk
  • Intercom
  • Custom webhooks
