Overview

Automate invoice processing with AI-powered document extraction. Perfect for accounts receivable teams handling high volumes of invoices.

Features

  • PDF Parsing: Extract data from scanned and digital PDFs
  • Field Detection: Automatically identify vendor, amount, date, line items
  • CSV Export: One-click export to spreadsheet-ready format
  • Batch Processing: Handle hundreds of invoices at once

Supported Formats

  • PDF (scanned & digital)
  • Images (PNG, JPG)
  • Email attachments
