Overview

A comprehensive legal research skill that helps attorneys and legal professionals quickly find relevant case law, statutes, and regulations.

Features

  • Precedent Search: Find relevant case law across multiple jurisdictions
  • Citation Assembly: Automatically format citations in Bluebook style
  • Brief Analysis: Analyze opposing briefs and suggest counter-arguments
  • Timeline Generation: Create case timelines from documents

Supported Jurisdictions

  • Federal Courts
  • All 50 US States
  • EU Courts (beta)
